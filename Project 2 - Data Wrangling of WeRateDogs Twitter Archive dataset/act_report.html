Analyzing & visualizing WeRateDogs twitter archive data¶

This report will infer from the insights and displayed visualizations produced from WeRateDogs twitter archive data Wrangling and Analyzing project.

Dog_stages by count

From the table above, it is evident that majority of the WeRateDogs dogs are in pupper stage by 65%, followed by doggo stage and least popular dog stage doggo-floofer.

Top 10 Dog Names

The top most dog names will be Tucker, Charlie, Oliver and Lucy with 9 dogs named as these followed by Penny with 8 dogs and last Koda among the top 10 dog names.

Relationship between Favorite and Retweet

The above plot shows a positive correlation between favorite(likes) count and retweet count posted by WeRateDogs. As the favorite count tends to increase the retweet count increases. Having see that a heatmap is plotted below to examine how strong the positive correlation between favorite count and retweet count is.

From the heatmap plot above, shows a very strong positive correlation between favorite count and retweet count by 93% or vice versa.
